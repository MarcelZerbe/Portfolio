Garbage Rock

"Garbage Rock" is a Concept Art Project based on a Short Story with the same name. It tells the story of an old hacker in a dystopian future, explaining how he managed to break out of a Prison chain called the garbage rocks.

The following pictures show the storyboard, character designs, self-made 3D references, and the finished frames.
